A ranking of my favorite current high fashion designers based on Twitter user sentiment, powered by Machine Learning

As consumer preferences continue to shift post-COVID and new generations enter the market, luxury brands must seek out creative and compelling ways to engage their audience. By using high fashion creative directors and their work as a proxy for brands’ ability to communicate their offering to consumers, I hope to provide meaningful insights into these brand-consumer relationships.


This tool was coded by yours truly using NLP and ML techniques. I plan to update data monthly and fine-tune/automate the model, as time allows. For technical (and interested) readers, more detailed information about my process can be found HERE.
